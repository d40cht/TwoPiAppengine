About TwoPi

  • TwoPI is a bit different from most route planning sites.
  • Just tell us where you want to start, how far you want to go and whether you're walking or cycling and we'll find you an endless variety of different routes.
  • By mixing a large number of freely available geographic data sets, we automatically generate circular routes that take you along the most appropriate and pleasant streets, paths or bike tracks, past interesting sites and through attractive places.
  • Having chosen a route, or set of routes you can export to any GPS unit that supports GPX routes and get going.
